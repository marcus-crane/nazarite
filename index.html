Samson, a man imbued with incredible strength due to his connection with his God and his vow as a Nazarite.

A vow to never cut his hair, nor let any wine touch his lips.

Delilah, his lover. Samson had endured much and she eased his pain.

Though she was not as she seemed; Deception lay behind those eyes.

Betrayed by Delilah, his hair was cut from his head.

His eyes were taken, and his strength destroyed.

Samson was sold to his enemies to work until his dying days.

Brought to a great temple to entertain those who enslaved him, Samson exacted his revenge.

With a final prayer spoken and a final prayer heard, a temple was brought to ruin.

And then, nothing.
